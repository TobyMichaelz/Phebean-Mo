About Us

The concept behind this wonderful event planning and management business originated from the desire to assist clients in receiving value for their money and crafting unforgettable memories!
Phebean Mo Event Consultants was established in October 2017 with a commitment to delivering top-notch event services to all our valued clients.
Our team of event planning and management professionals is committed to executing high-quality events of different scales and varieties. We aim to deliver an exceptional experience for our clients and their guests, starting from the beginning all the way to the end.
